Steak Burrito



A classical recipe of the Mexican Food, a combination of steak, rice and vegetetables on a tortilla.


Ingredients

  • 1 (10-inch) flour tortilla
  • 3/4 cup cubed, cooked ribeye steak, warmed
  • 1/4 cup Mexican rice, heated
  • 1/4 cup pinto beans, heated
  • 1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon diced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Fold a paper towel around tortilla. Microwave on High until pliable, 5 to 10 seconds.
  2. Lay tortilla flat on a plate.
  3. Layer steak, rice, beans, cheese, onion, and cilantro in a line across the middle of the tortilla.
  4. Fold opposing edges of the tortilla to overlap the filling.
  5. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling and tuck tortilla to form a burrito.
